Peanut Butter, Jelly Time

  1. Get 2 pieces of bread, PB, Jelly and a knife
  2. Use knife to spread PB on one side of one piece of bread
  3. Use knife to spread jelly on one side of the other piece of bread
  4. Put PB side and Jelly side together
  5. Squish so they stick
